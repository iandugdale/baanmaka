Chalet rooms

We have 11 chalet rooms. There are five bungalows housing two separate en-suite twin rooms with a large shared balcony and another single bungalow with one en-suite double room and balcony.

All 11 chalet rooms are bookable individually.

Price - 1400 Baht per night. 

House rooms

We have a house with three separate en-suite rooms. There are two twin rooms and one double room.

All 3 house rooms are bookable individually.

Price - 1400 Baht per night.
