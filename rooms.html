Bungalow rooms

We have 11 bungalow rooms. There are five bungalows housing two separate en-suite rooms with a large shared balcony and another single bungalow with one en-suite room and balcony.

All 11 bungalow rooms are bookable individually.

Price - 1400 Baht per night. 

House

We have a two bedroom house with a large lounge, a kitchen and a balcony. This suits a family or a group of friends travelling together.
