Baan Maka is a high end homestay dedicated to making your visit to Kaeng Krachan as enjoyable and rewarding as possible.

We offer comfortable rooms with air conditioning and a restaurant specialising in local and regional Thai dishes. The lodge is situated  a few kilometres from Kaeng Krachan NP in three hectares of gardens and forest.
